Hannah Feehan

Classical Guitarist

Hannah is a classical guitarist based in York, UK.

Primarily, Hannah works as a teacher of guitar and music theory, but does perform as a soloist, as well as composes and arranged music for the classical guitar and other ensembles

Her students regularly achieve great successes in competitions and examinations. Some highlights include on student performing on The West End as Zak in School of Rock, and others have gained places in the prestigious National Youth Guitar Ensemble.
